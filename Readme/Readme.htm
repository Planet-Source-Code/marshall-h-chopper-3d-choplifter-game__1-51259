Welcome to the Readme file for Chopper!

Your mission:

You start out from the US embassy in Saudi Arabia. You need to rescue the 16 hostages across the river.

To rescue them, shoot their house door until it opens and the people come out. Then land and they will get into your helicopter. You can only carry 4 at a time, so you will have to make trips back to the embassy. Land at the embassy and wait for the people to safely enter the building. Repeat until all people are saved.

Be careful, though! Tanks guard all the houses and they will shoot for you. Don't let them hit you, you only have 3 lives. You can shoot them, but be warned that there will be more next time you come back.

Also, watch out for the fighter plane that shoots you.

When I start the game, it says
AUTOMATION ERROR.

The most likely thing is your video card does not support 640x480 at 16 bit color. If it doesn't, you must have a really old computer.


The game runs really s l o w .

You probably don't have a great 3D accelerator card, or you need to change some of the settings for the game. Run Config.exe and select a low fullscreen resolution, like 320x240


I get Object Variable or With block not set

You need to get DirectX, see below


The sound is really choppy.

You probably don't have the latest DirectX drivers for your sound card. Go to http://www.microsoft.com/directx
